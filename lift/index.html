What’s Included

A quick overview of my Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) process for designing landing pages is as follows:

1. Research and Analysis

The information gained here provides the foundation for all work to follow. I study the information you send me, search the web for additional information, analyse your website and review your competitors websites.

2. Direct Response Copywriting

Great copy is the workhorse behind your conversions and this paired with the right design can amplify your conversions with a cumulative effect.

3. Design

Based on my research and analysis, I create wireframes that outline the structure and layout of your new page/s. I then work with my expert team of designers to design the new page based on my wireframes and copy.

4. Development (optional)

Each design is carefully crafted pixel by pixel into clean HTML, CSS and Javascript code and then tested across the four main browsers (Safari, Firefox, Chrome & IE). We also provide ‘Responsive design’ as standard so that your pages look great on tablets and mobiles.

FAQ

Q: How many concepts or mockups do you design?
A: Just the one unless we’ve agreed otherwise, but it’s the one I believe is the best to increase your conversions, so much so that I offer a 80% money back guarantee.

Q: How many revisions do I get on my design?
A: You may have up to 3 revisions on the copy and 3 revisions on the design.

Q: What if I don’t like the finished design?
A: This rarely happens as we work closely with you throughout the process, adjusting the design based on your feedback. If you still don’t like the design AND it’s doesn’t increase your conversion rate, then I have no problem trying again with a new design or giving you a 80% refund.

Q: How do we tell if the new design worked?
A: We run what’s called an A/B test between the old design and the new design. When we run the test, the traffic is split evenly between the old and new landing pages and any conversions are recorded.

Q: So what happens if your design doesn’t increase my conversion rate?
A: We try again. I analyse the results of the first A/B test and create a new design. This is all included and free of charge. If we repetitively fail to increase your conversion rate (which hasn’t happened yet) then we offer you the 80% refund.

Q: Can you code the new design as well?
A: Yes, if required each design can be carefully crafted pixel by pixel into clean HTML, CSS and Javascript code and then tested across the four main browsers (Safari, Firefox, Chrome & IE). We also provide ‘Responsive design’ as standard so that your pages look great on tablets and mobiles.

Q: Can you integrate the new design into my CMS?
A: Yes we can. Just let me know the details of your CMS and I will include the template development for your CMS in your quote.

Q: Do you offer hosting for the landing page?
A: No, but I’m happy to recommend a hosting provider to fit your needs.
