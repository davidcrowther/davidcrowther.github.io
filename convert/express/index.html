Think your website could be working harder at generating leads and sales?

You know your website could be performing better, but you’re unsure exactly what to do to improve it …

Convert Express is my service where I analyse your website and put together a written report that outlines the steps you need to take to increase your website’s conversion rate.

You can use the report to make immediate improvements to your website, or to create a list of ideas to run your own A/B tests.

Convert Express is like a website audit and focuses on:

  • Goals: Are your business objectives and website objectives in alignment? Are visitors to your website taking the actions you want them to take?

  • Usability: Are there any issues that are creating friction, or completely blocking your visitors from achieving their goals on your website?

  • Sales Funnel: Is the journey your visitors take to complete their goals on your website working seamlessly, or are you losing customers because your sales funnel has a leak?

  • Brand: Your business name, logo, the design of your website, and the tone of your copy all point to the personality of your company. How are you coming across to your users?

  • USP (unique selling point): Are you pushing the benefits you provide that make you stand out from your competition?

  • Pricing Strategy: This is one of the most critical factors in your application’s potential success. Do you know how to make your pricing work for you, instead of against you?

  • Copywriting: Copy is the workhorse behind your conversions, and it’s essential that you get it right across the whole of your application—from your home page to your notification emails.

  • Shopping Cart and Checkout: Is your shopping cart and checkout process smooth and painless for your customers? What’s causing people to abandon your checkout process, costing you sales?

  • Forms: Are your forms usable? Do they clearly describe what fields are required and which ones are optional? Does the form validation indicate clearly where the user went wrong in filling out the form?

  • Search: How accurate are your search results? Can your users easily filter and order their search results?
