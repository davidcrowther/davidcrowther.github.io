An Overview of Our Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Design Process

1. Research and Analysis:

Define your business and website goals, evaluate your website, study the information you send us, search the web for additional information, analyse your website analytics and review your competitors websites.

2. Information Architecture:

Information architecture is the art and science of figuring out what you want your site to do and then constructing a plan of how we’ll achieve that goal. We’ll create a site map and sometimes user journeys (flowcharts) when required.

3. Direct Response Copywriting

Great copy is the workhorse behind your conversions and this paired with the right design can amplify your conversions with a cumulative effect.

4. Design:

Based on our research and analysis, We’ll create wireframes that outline the structure and layout of your new pages. The wireframes are then transformed into beautiful and functional designs.

5. Development (optional)

The new design is carefully crafted pixel by pixel into clean HTML, CSS and Javascript code and then tested across the four main browsers (Safari, Firefox, Chrome & IE). We also offer ‘Responsive design’ so that your pages look and work great on tablet and mobile devices.

FAQ

What is a site map?
A site map defines the structure and hierarchy of your website. It also creates the taxonomy, or the names for your websites sections and pages.

What is a user journey?
A user journey, usually presented as a flow chart, shows the anticipated or ideal path the visitor will take travelling through your website. It’s important to work out how the lead or sales funnel will work to optimize conversions.

How many concepts or mockups do you design?
Just the one per page, but it’s the one we believe is the best to increase your conversions.

How many revisions do I get for each wireframe or design?
You may have up to 3 revisions on the copy, 3 revisions on each wireframe and 3 revisions on each design. Additional revisions can still be requested, but will incur an additional fee.

What if I don’t like the finished design?
Design is a personal thing and everyone has their opinions of what looks good. We design based on data and results and that takes precedence over certain design trends. If you feel this will be an issue, we may not be the best fit for you.

How do we tell if the new design worked?
If you have over 10,000 monthly visitors, we run what’s called an A/B test between the old design and the new design. When we run the test, the traffic is split evenly between the old and new website and any conversions are recorded.

So what happens if your design doesn’t increase my conversion rate?
If you qualify for our guarantee and we’ve tested your new design against the old, we’ll analyse the results of the first A/B test and create you a new design. This is all included and free of charge.

Can you code the new design as well?
Yes, if required each design can be carefully crafted pixel by pixel into clean HTML, CSS and Javascript code and then tested across the four main browsers (Safari, Firefox, Chrome & IE). We also provide ‘Responsive design’ as standard so that your pages look and work great on tablets and mobiles.

Can you integrate the new design into my CMS?
Yes we can. Just let us know the details of your CMS and we’ll include it in your quote.

Do you offer website hosting?
No, but we’re happy to recommend a hosting provider to fit your needs.
