FAQ

Q: What is a site map?
A: A site map defines the structure and hierarchy of your website. It also creates the taxonomy, or the names for your websites sections and pages.

Q: What is a user journey?
A: A user journey, usually presented as a flow chart, shows the anticipated or ideal path the visitor will take travelling through your website. It’s important to work out how the lead or sales funnel will work to optimize conversions.

Q: How many concepts or mockups do you design?
A: Just the one per page, but it’s the one I believe is the best to increase your conversions, so much so that I offer a 80% money back guarantee.

Q: How many revisions do I get for each wireframe or design?
A: You may have up to 3 revisions on the copy, 3 revisions on each wireframe and 3 revisions on each design.

Q: What if I don’t like the finished design?
A: This rarely happens as we work closely with you throughout the process, adjusting the design based on your feedback. If you still don’t like the design AND it’s doesn’t increase your conversion rate, then I have no problem trying again with a new design or giving you a 80% refund.

Q: How do we tell if the new design worked?
A: We run what’s called an A/B test between the old design and the new design. When we run the test, the traffic is split evenly between the old and new website and any conversions are recorded.

Q: So what happens if your design doesn’t increase my conversion rate?
A: We try again. I analyse the results of the first A/B test and create a new design. This is all included and free of charge. If we repetitively fail to increase your conversion rate (which hasn’t happened yet) then we offer you the 80% refund.

Q: Can you code the new design as well?
A: Yes, if required each design can be carefully crafted pixel by pixel into clean HTML, CSS and Javascript code and then tested across the four main browsers (Safari, Firefox, Chrome & IE). We also provide ‘Responsive design’ as standard so that your pages look great on tablets and mobiles.

Q: Can you integrate the new design into my CMS?
A: Yes we can. Just let me know the details of your CMS and I will include the template development for your CMS in your quote.

Q: Do you offer website hosting?
A: No, but I’m happy to recommend a hosting provider to fit your needs.
