I had a good look around your website and instead of jumping straight in with a full website redesign, I propose a slightly different approach.

But first, a quick question for you: you offer a free Resume Critique and I would like to know how well they convert into sales, verses the conversion rate for direct sales.

The reason I ask is your answer may change a part of my proposed solution.

 Anyway … my recommendation to you is we design a new sales funnel with the following steps:

  1. Home page / landing page
  2. Upsell page
  3. Discount upsell page
  4. Payment page
  5. Order confirmation page

So the new home page will include content from the current home page, other parts of your website like the pricing, and new content I will create myself.

The whole focus of this new page will to lead them to the next step in the sales funnel, the upsell page.

The upsell page offers the customer the various add-on services you provide that are currently displayed at http://www.professional-resumes.com/services-and-prices.shtml

If the customer does not add any additional services on the upsell page, they are taken to a second ‘discount’ upsell page where they are offered the add-on services at one time offer discount.

From there they go to the payment page and finally the order comfirmation page which completes the funnel.

The whole goal of this new process is to maximize profit per sale as well as increase overall sales per visitor.
