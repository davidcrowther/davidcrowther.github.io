How does it work?

Our proven process for managing PPC campaigns is as follows:

  1. Website and landing pages analysis
  2. Campaign and ad group structure analysis and optimization (existing campaigns)
  3. Competition analysis (ad texts, keywords, bidding)
  4. Keyword research
  5. Create tightly-themed keyword lists
  6. Add relevant ad copy by professional copywriter to each of the keyword lists
  7. Additional features if required (ad extensions, remarketing, mobile campaigns)
  8. Network distribution
  9. Analytics set-up and configuration of conversion goals
  10. Campaign launch
  11. Data analysis and ongoing campaign optimization (search term report, conversion optimization, CTR optimization, CPC optimization, landing page optimization)

We don’t work with the following:

  • You have an advertising budget (ad spend) of less than $1,000 per month.
  • You’re selling products or services you don’t own (affiliate marketing).
  • You’re selling a restricted product or service—see https://support.google.com/adwordspolicy/topic/1308252

Our requirements:

  • You have analytics installed and are tracking conversions. If you need help in this I can recommend a trusted professional that I use.
  • If you display a phone number on your landing page and measure success as visitors to your landing page calling you, then call tracking must be installed. I recommend calltrackingmetrics.com or callrail.com if you don’t currently have a provider.

FAQ

What should my Adwords budget be?
Not sure where to start? We recommend starting at a smaller number you feel comfortable with (min. $1,000/m) and growing from there. Online advertising is all about constantly improving metrics. As you see your campaigns improve you can choose to increase your budget.

Do you give refunds?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money back guarantee. We want you to love our service. It can take a couple of months for a campaign to settle in and start operating in the black. But after 60 days, if you’re not satisfied with our service and the results we’re generating, I’ll happily refund 80% of our fee (does not include any advertising budget spent).

Can your experts help with Remarketing, Display Network & Product Listing Ads?
Yes. We’ll run whichever types of campaigns we think will be best for your business. That’s not to say we’ll run all of these types of campaigns from day-one. Good advertising is about testing, measuring and iterating on the channels that work.

Do I need an Adwords account?
You don’t need an Adwords account before working with us, but you’ll require an account before you start your first campaign. It takes no more than 30 seconds to create an Adwords account and we’re happy to show you how.

Do you offer advertising on any other platform?
We work with the following advertising platforms: Google Adwords, Bing Ads, LinkedIn Ads and Facebook Ads. If you wish to advertise on one of these alternative platforms, please contact me for a custom quote.
